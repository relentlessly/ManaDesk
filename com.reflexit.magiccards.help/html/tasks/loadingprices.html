Online Price is a price of cards of a card for sale by a given provider. ManaDesk supports two prices columns - Online Price (SPrice) - on the Scryfall database cards, and user price on owned instances of these cards. First one is for how much you can buy, and second one for how much your actually bought it.

Price provider

Currently, only TCGPlayer is supported. The price is extracted when the database is updated from Scryfall website.
